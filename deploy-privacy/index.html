GymWaBot — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

GymWaBot collects the following information from gym members as provided by the gym owner:

  • Name and phone number
  • Gym membership plan details (plan type, start/end dates, fees)
  • Payment transaction records
  • Attendance records

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Send membership renewal reminders via WhatsApp
  • Generate and send payment links for gym membership fees
  • Track gym attendance
  • Provide gym management analytics to the gym owner

3. WhatsApp Messaging

We use the Meta WhatsApp Business API to send automated messages to gym members. Messages include membership reminders, payment links, and attendance confirmations. Members can opt out of WhatsApp messages at any time by informing their gym owner.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely in encrypted cloud databases (MongoDB Atlas). We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for all data transmission. Payment processing is handled entirely by Razorpay; we do not store any card or UPI details.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties except:

  • Razorpay — for payment link generation (name, phone)
  • Meta/WhatsApp — for sending WhatsApp messages (phone number)
  • Google Gemini AI — for generating personalized reminder messages (first name only, no phone)

6. Data Retention

Member data is retained as long as the gym owner maintains an active account. Gym owners can delete individual member records at any time from the dashboard.
